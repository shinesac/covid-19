COVID-19 Company Policy & Expectations

We are excited and happy to welcome everyone back. As we start to open back up, we relieze the importance of transparency and open communication during these uncertain times. We will use this website to post relevant updates and as a central resource for matters relating to COVID-19. This website also serves to update you with live accounting of potential and confirmed COVID-19 cases within our facility. We hope that this information will help encourage and guide nescessary actions to ensure the health and safety of our employees.

Requirements

  • Report all COVI9-19 suspected illness and absenses through this portal.
  • Supervisors: Confirm absenses through this portal.
  • All employees must maintain 6 feet apart and wear face masks.

What if I Feel Sick?

If you find yourself feeling ill with any COVID-19 symptoms, we ask that you do not report to work. All COVID-19 related absences will be excused and paid during this time. Please see the CDC guidelines for further information on COVID-19.

Concerns & Suggestions

Please use the form above to submit any concerns or suggestions for COVID-19. We welcome any suggestions to make our workplace a safer environment and look forward to your input. Please also use this form to report non-compliance with COVID-19 policy. You may also call our dedicated hotline, Mon-Fri between 8am-6pm EST, for COVID-19 matters at 1-888-888-8888.
